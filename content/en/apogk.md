## About APOGK

The Portuguese Association of Okinawa Goju-Ryu Karate-Do (A.P.O.G.K.) was founded in 1982 and is an association affiliated with the I.O.G.K.F. (International Okinawa Goju-Ryu Karate-Do Federation).

**Official Website**: [https://apogk.pt](https://apogk.pt)

### Mission and Objectives

The association aims to:

1. **Promote and disseminate** the practice of Okinawa Goju-Ryu Karate-Do as a formative activity and sport modality, as well as protect its reputation through the establishment of applicable legal procedures against those who offend it.

2. **Organize and participate** in training seminars, tournaments, and national and regional championships.

3. **Conduct and validate** graduation examinations.

4. **Develop cooperative relationships** with similar associations, national or foreign, particularly through membership in federations or unions.

5. **Safeguard the interests** of its members.

6. **Promote the practice** of other sports modalities.

## Sensei Jorge Monteiro - Chief Instructor

Jorge Monteiro, National Chief Instructor, was born on September 17, 1961, in the city of Gaia. In 1974 he began training Karate Shotokai at a scout club, then switched to training Shotokan in September 1976.

### Early Years and Introduction to Goju-Ryu

In 1977, he met Sensei Morio Higaonna at a seminar in the city of Porto, from which he began practicing Goju-Ryu. It was also at this seminar that he obtained 1st place in both Kumite and Kata at the first Goju-Ryu championship in Portugal.

In 1979, he participated in the first international Goju-Ryu Gasshuku in England, during which the I.O.G.K.F. (International Okinawa Goju-Ryu Karate-Do Federation) was founded. In 1980, he was graded 1st Dan by Sensei Morio Higaonna during a European seminar in Spain.

### Career Achievements

**Competition and Recognition:**

- In 1984, he was selected by the National Karate Federation and participated in the 19th European Championship of WUKO (World Union Karate-Do Organisation) in France
- In 1987, he graduated as a 2nd Grade Instructor by the Martial Arts Directive Committee

**Leadership Roles:**

- In 1988, he was chosen as Chief Instructor of Portugal by Sensei Morio Higaonna
- In 2000, he was appointed member of the Shikoin Kai, Executive Committee of IOGKF, in Sweden
- In 2004, he was awarded the title of Shihan by Sensei Morio Higaonna

**Officiating and Recent Achievements:**

- Has refereed several times in national championships of F.P.K.D.A. (Portuguese Federation of Karate and Associated Disciplines), F.N.K.-P. (National Federation of Karate – Portugal), and in IOGKF world championships
- Currently holds the rank of Karate Coach – Grade IV
- More recently, in 2015, he traveled to Okinawa for the IOGKF Chief Instructors Seminar held every two years, where he was graded 8th Dan

### Legacy and Dedication

Sensei Jorge Monteiro represents the dedication and authentic transmission of Goju-Ryu karate in Portugal, maintaining the high standards established by his teacher, Sensei Morio Higaonna, and ensuring the continuation of traditional Okinawan karate practices through A.P.O.G.K.
